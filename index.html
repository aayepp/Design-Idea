  1. Open Google Gemini - Go to gemini.google.com
  2. Start New Chat - Click "New chat" to create a fresh conversation
  3. Prompt for Video Generation - Paste your prompt with this prefix: "Using your video generation capabilities, create a video from this design prompt:"
  4. Paste Design Prompt - Add your full design prompt text
  5. Request Video Output - Add: "Generate a video representation of this design"
  6. Wait for Processing - Gemini will process and generate video
  7. Download or Share - Get your video link or download
Visit Google Gemini →
  1. Access DaVinci.AI - Open davinci.ai in your browser
  2. Create Video Project - Click "New Video" or "Create Project"
  3. Choose Video Mode - Select "Text-to-Video" or "AI Generation"
  4. Paste Your Design Prompt - Click in the text area and paste your full prompt
  5. Set Video Parameters - Choose duration (30s, 60s, etc), resolution, and FPS
  6. Add Music (Optional) - Select background music if desired
  7. Generate Video - Click "Generate" button and wait for processing
  8. Edit & Export - Fine-tune if needed and export your video
Visit DaVinci.AI →
  1. Visit Kling.AI - Go to kling.ai website
  2. Sign In/Register - Create account or login to your existing account
  3. Start Generation - Click "Create Video" or "New Generation"
  4. Select Model - Choose Kling model version available
  5. Paste Your Prompt - Enter your design prompt in the prompt field
  6. Configure Duration - Set video length (5s, 10s recommended for posters)
  7. Generate Video - Click "Generate" and wait for video creation
  8. Download Output - Download the generated video in your preferred format
